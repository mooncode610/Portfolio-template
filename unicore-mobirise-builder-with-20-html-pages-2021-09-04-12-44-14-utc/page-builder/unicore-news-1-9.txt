Unicore Page Builder v1.9

What's new?
* Compatibility with Mobirise 4.x
* Bug fixes with drop down menu.

How to Upgrade:
1. Download the LATEST version of Mobirise from https://mobirise.com/
2. Download the theme from your Themeforest account http://themeforest.net/downloads
3. Unzip unicore-x-x.mbrext and import to Mobirise "Menu->Extensions&Themes->Import"
4. Restart the Mobirise app.
More info: http://electricblaze.com/unicore/page-builder/help.html
